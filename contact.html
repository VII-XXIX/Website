❓ Frequently Asked Questions

📱 How do I book a gaming slot?

The easiest way is through WhatsApp at +91 7448902644. You can also call us or use our online booking form. We respond quickly to WhatsApp messages!

⏰ What are your operating hours?

We're open daily from 10:00 AM to 10:00 PM. Last booking is accepted at 9:00 PM to ensure you get adequate gaming time.

🚶 Do you accept walk-ins?

Yes, but we recommend booking in advance, especially on weekends. Walk-ins are subject to availability and may have to wait during peak hours.

Yes! We provide free parking for all our customers. The parking area is secure and well-lit for your convenience.

Outside food is allowed, but we also have a variety of snacks and beverages available for purchase. Please keep the gaming area clean!

Absolutely! We host birthday parties, corporate events, and gaming tournaments. Contact us for custom packages and pricing.
